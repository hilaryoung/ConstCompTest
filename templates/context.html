Context
As part of our ongoing efforts to enhance the Constraint Monitor tool in OBP, we are introducing a new Constraint Component. This component is designed to display key details about constraints, including: Metered Status, System Tag, Margin, and Time to Breach. Today we are specifically testing the UI of Margin between Limit and Transfer, and time to breach.

The goal of creating the Constraint Component is to improve visibility into constraint conditions whenever they appear in the OBP UI. With this component we hope to:
  • Help users more easily identify and prioritise critical constraints
  • Streamline constraints related operational decision-making
  • Improve the overall monitoring and management of constraint-related issues across the platform
